Tone and Voice

The feeling Coolbet customers get when seeing our content (texts, photos, videos, promotions, company events, etc.) is catchy, inspiring, innovative, fun, welcoming, and always responsible.

Coolbet has the same voice everywhere; it’s just the tone that changes depending on the occasion.

Coolbet voice:

  • Plainspoken -We value clarity above all. All kinds of slang and trendy metaphors are left to speak with your friends, not with our customers.
  • Genuine -We care about our customers. That means we relate to customers’ challenges and passions and speak to them in a familiar, warm, and welcoming way.
  • Humorous -Humor brings a smile to people's faces. We use it sparingly, dropping in a timely and appropriate lighthearted joke just to ease the tension and lighten the mood. We never insult anyone and prefer subtly to laugh-out-loud.

Coolbet tone:

Coolbet's tone is informal, but always very clear and understandable. When writing content or answering a request/email, we think ahead and try to anticipate what the reader's state of mind might be. Once we have an idea of their emotional state, we adjust our tone accordingly.

Guidelines

Tips to keep in mind when speaking on behalf of Coolbet:

  • Stay cool, but not too familiar. Don’t use slang that is not understandable for everyone. Don’t be too formal either.
  • Be involved, especially when you are in direct contact with customers. We don’t copy-paste replies containing useless information. We put ourselves into our customers’ shoes and provide them with insightful replies.
  • Stay humble. Behind the Coolbet brand are humans who care about our customers.
  • Stay positive.
