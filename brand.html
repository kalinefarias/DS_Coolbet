Brand

First of all, what means brand for us?

Is a result: customers gut feeling about a product, service, or company. It ends up in their head, in their hearts. Customers create the brand from all the raw material we throw at them. We are not creating one brand, we are creating millions of brands (in every customer's head is a different brand)

Is reputation: Brand is a result of our actions. What we are putting out through the design, messages, looks and feels, culture, our behavior. Everyone in the company affects the brand.

That's why all employees need to be synced.

Coolbet Brand Guidelines

Coolbet is all about momentum and innovation, and that’s why we are strongly focused on product development and setting new standards for responsible gaming. We work hard every day to provide services and products of high quality.

And our brand follows the same purpose.


Breaking Coolbet Brand

  • Name
    • The name Coolbet was chosen for two reasons. Firstly, because it’s short and impossible to forget. Secondly, because it represents our spirit “Stay Cool and Bet Responsibly”.

  • Logo
    • Very unique colored logo.
    • So it's a good approach to stand out more. And be recognizable.

  • Colors and Typography
    • Green, Blue, Orange, Red. Each color represents one of our main values Transparency, Responsibility, Quality Innovation.
    • So it's a good approach to stand out through shapes, components, details.
    • Roboto is our main font because is clear, has easy adaptation, and is great for mobile.

  • Our mascot bears (artsy styled characters)
    • Helps us to keep fancy playful way conversation to customer.
    • Bring trust and uniqueness.
    • It is a tool to keep our brand regional and consistent at the same time.

  • Tone and Voice
    • Needs to be clear, readable, and understandable quickly.

Marketing Values

Each Color in Coolbet logo represent one of our core values and that’s why it is so important to use them with pride:


Transparent

Coolbet allows customers to see all their activity whenever they want - both wins and losses. Coolbet talks openly about the future. Coolbet is familiar with all our customers. We are very transparent about all the risks that gambling brings with itself.


Responsible

Stay Cool, Bet Responsibly. It’s our duty to make sure each customer can personally afford such entertainment as gambling in the range they have chosen. Afford not only financially but also mentally. .


Quality

Whatever we do, there are no compromises in quality. We strive for best customer service, because our customers are most important to us. We offer the best products which we develop every day, and make them even better. Whenever we have new activities, outdoor or on site, we make sure they are executed perfectly.


Innovation

Our 300+ employees make sure that Coolbet never gets boring, working on new cool features and opportunities is in our DNA.

Marketing Vision

Deliver outstanding product, continuous innovation, exceptional service and non-stop entertainment.

Purpose

Offer the best quality thrilling entertainment in a responsible way

Personality

Catchy, inspiring, quality, fun and always responsible.

Tagline

Stay cool. Bet responsibly.
