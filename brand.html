Brand

First of all, what does brand mean for us?

It is a result: Brand makes customers feel good about a product, service or company. The brand sticks in their head, in their hearts. Customers create the brand from all the raw material we expose them to. We are not creating one brand, we are creating millions of brands (in every customer's head is a different brand).

It is a reputation: Brand is a result of our actions. What we are putting out through the design, messages, looks and feels, culture, our behavior. Everyone in the company affects the brand.

That's why all employees need to be in sync.

Coolbet Brand Guidelines

Coolbet is all about momentum and innovation. That’s why we are strongly focused on product development and setting new standards for responsible gaming. We work hard every day to provide high quality services and products. It’s the same for our brand. To quote Jeff Bezos, A brand for a company is like a reputation for a person. You earn reputation by trying to do hard things well.”

And our brand follows the same purpose.


Coolbet Brand

  • Name
    • The name Coolbet was chosen for two reasons. Firstly, because it’s short and impossible to forget. Secondly, because it represents our spirit “Stay Cool and Bet Responsibly”.

  • Logo
    • Colorful, fun, memorable, and full of energy.
    • It's a good approach to stand out more and be recognizable.
    • Let's keep it visible.

  • Colors and Typography
    • Green, Blue, Orange, Red. Each color represents one of our main values Responsibility, Transparency, Quality, Innovation.
    • It's a good approach to stand out through shapes, components and details.
    • Roboto is our main font because is clear, has easy adaptation, and is great for mobile.

  • Our mascot bears (artsy-styled characters)
    • Helps us maintain a fancy, playful way to converse with the customer.
    • Brings trust and uniqueness.
    • It is a tool to keep our brand regional and consistent at the same time.

  • Tone and Voice
    • Needs to be clear, readable and quickly understandable.

Marketing Values

Each color used in the Coolbet logo has a meaning and represents one core value.


Responsibility

Stay Cool, Bet Responsibly. It is our duty to check that each customer can personally afford entertainment such as gambling, both financially and psychologically.


Transparency

Coolbet gives customers an opportunity to see all of their activity whenever they want - both wins and losses. Coolbet talks openly about its future plans. Coolbet is familiar with all of our customers. Adding to that, we are very transparent about the risks that come with gambling.


Quality

Whatever we do, there are no compromises on quality. We strive for the best customer service, because our customers are important to us. We offer the best products, which we improve every day to make them even better. Whenever we host new activities, outdoor or on site, we make sure they are executed perfectly.


Innovation

Our 300+ employees make sure that Coolbet never gets boring. Developing cool new features and opportunities is in our DNA.

Marketing Vision

Deliver an outstanding product, continuous innovation, exceptional service, and non-stop entertainment.

Purpose

Offer top-quality and thrilling entertainment in a responsible way.

Personality

Catchy, inspiring, quality, fun and always responsible.

Tagline

Stay cool. Bet responsibly.
