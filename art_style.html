Background

Backgrounds are largely used by the company. It help to put the focus on the key points of the campaign.


Size and Guideline

Make a background is not so easy as seems. The image need to express the subject of the campaign but not get more attencions them the content. Coolbet backgrounds in general are more dark to combine with the platform.

When we talk about the disposition of the elements, it is needed pay attencion to the different medias the image will be used, as sizes of screens, tv, mobile (cellphone, ipad), social media formats, etc.

  • Size: 1920x1200px
  • Format: .webp or .jpg
  • Important: The last layer need to be a bottom gradient.
    Gradient: #191919 - 100% bottom to 0% up.

    Code:
    background: rgb(25,25,25);
    background: linear-gradient(0deg, rgba(25,25,25,1) 0%, rgba(25,25,25,0) 100%);

Sport

Tips: Backgrounds for sports in general need to be dinamic and reconazible.

There are many ways to do dinamic images, play with geometric shapes, blur motion filter and energetic main image, are some of them.

Each campaign request specifities, sometimes is about the choosen sport and sometimes is about the country. For this case flags, sport elements(balls, uniform, stadium, main spot tools, etc).


Poker

Tips: Most of poker's campaigns came from the providers, that means you will keep the visuals and identity sent by the provider.

If the campaign don't came by providers, you can use poker elements like cards, coins, chips.
