The Coolbet brand has been a success

In part because of our image styles. While competitors bet on photos, Coolbet uses a unique painted image style that highlights our very engaging mascot, the CoolBear.

Painting

The main Coolbet trait is our painted images. This style brings uniqueness to each channel that we advertise on.

Coolbet rarely uses real images, like photos of the athletes or generic real person images.



Symbols

There are also sport symbols to use. The symbols are an element or a combination of elements that brings to the customers’ minds the idea of a specific sport. Important: It is not permitted to use the official brands from teams or players' images.


Vector

In a few cases it is good to have vector images, like printed merch materials, a casino campaign, championship patterns, or backgrounds.

Bears

The Coolbear is our mascot and an important tool for being memorable.

The bear looks like a sport: active, entertaining, fun, friendly, and competitive.

It is represented as a humanoid bear to make it easier for customers to identify with it. Also, the Coolbear can be represented in human poses, in a sports environment, and with human emotions.

Background

Backgrounds are widely used by the company. It helps to put the focus on the key points of each campaign.


Size and Guideline

Making a background is not as easy as it seems. The image needs to highlight the subject of the campaign without getting more attention than the content. In general, the backgrounds used at Coolbet are always dark.

When we talk about the disposition of the elements, it is necessary to pay attention to different media on which the image will appear in relation to different screen sizes such as TV, mobile - (cellphone, iPad), social media formats, etc.

  • Size: 1920x1200px
  • Format: .webp or .jpg
  • Important: The last layer needs to be a bottom gradient.
    Gradient: #191919 - 100% bottom to 0% up.

    Code:
    background: rgb(25,25,25);
    background: linear-gradient(0deg, rgba(25,25,25,1) 0%, rgba(25,25,25,0) 100%);

Sport

Tips: Backgrounds for sports in general need to be dinamic and reconazible.

There are many ways to create dynamic images. You can, for example, play with geometric shapes, blur motion filters, and/or make our main image of the Coolbear more energetic looking.

Each campaign has its own specifics. Sometimes those specifics are linked to the country – flags, for example, and sometimes to sport-related gear such as balls, uniforms, stadiums, sports equipment, etc.).



Poker

Tips:Most poker campaigns are created by providers, which means you will keep the visuals and identity that was sent by the provider.

If the campaign isn't created by providers, you can use poker elements like cards, coins, chips, etc.
