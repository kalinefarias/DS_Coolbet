General Guidelines

Coolbet always & only uses Roboto. All our materials, products, and digital communication use this font, only changing the weight, size, and family (Regular and Condensed).

Roboto has a dual nature. It has a mechanical skeleton and the forms are largely geometric. At the same time, the font features friendly and open curves. This makes for a more natural reading rhythm.

Typescale - Landing Pages

To create balanced landing pages and friendly typography experience, Coolbet has a font scale.

Hierarchy is communicated through differences in font weight (Light, Medium, Regular), size, letter spacing, and case.

The Coolbet scale is based on 16px = 1em.
