Colors

The Coolbet brand is colorful, fun, memorable, and full of energy.


Accents Colors

The colors are most frequently used across all media. These are usually the colors that a brand sets as its identity. Using brand colors as primary colors strengthens brand awareness.

As was explained before, Coolbet has four main colors and each of them is related to our values.


Review:
  • Blue: Transparency
  • Green: Responsibility
  • Orange: Quality
  • Red: Innovation

Supporting Colors

These complement the primary color. These are to be used sparingly and only to make the elements stand out.

Primary

For the Coolbet brand, the darker grays are important because they are the base for our product and materials.

TIP: Avoid using pure white (#fff) or pure black (#000).
