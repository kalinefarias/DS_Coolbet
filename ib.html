General Guidelines

Inside of the campaign assets, we have the internal banners. They are used to display the campaign inside our product. They can be shown in the lobby, hub pages, or in the poker software.

As the internals are related to a campaign, they need to have the same visual style as the campaign.

Promotion Lobby

Used internally for promotion, the lobby contains all components. Pay attention to the colors. They need to fit with the campaign color and need to be highlighted and or stand out when placed near all banners. So, avoid using the same colors side by side in the lobby.
