Welcome to Coolbet

Coolbet, the B2C brand by GAN Ltd is an own-developed gaming site and sportsbook software. Coolbet was launched in May 2016 and it has served over 700.000 unique customers. After establishing a strong footprint in the Nordics (Norway, Sweden, Finland, Iceland and Estonia), Coolbet also expanded to Canada, Chile, Ecuador and Peru. The Coolbet HQ is located in Estonia with over 300 employees. In January 2021 Coolbet became a part of GAN Limited, and is also referred to as Coolbet By Gan.

The success of Coolbet can be seen in the growth of the unique customers we serve nowadays in 9 different markets in Europe, Canada and Latin-America.

Coolbet is all about momentum and innovation, and that’s why we are strongly focused on product development and setting new standards for responsible gaming. We work hard every day to provide quality services, products, and, of course, execution.

The name Coolbet was chosen for two reasons. Firstly, because it’s short and impossible to forget. Secondly, because it represents our motto- “Stay Cool and Bet Responsibly”.

Products

Sportbook

Coolbet is an independently developed and in-house sustained sportbook platform. With unique selling points such as top market payback on odds, transparency and innovation, Coolbet separates itself from the competition.

Along with top odds, we also provide a top-class customer experience with a personalized sportbook lobby based on each customer's betting history. Similar to companies such as Netflix and Youtube, we offer our logged-in customers recommended betting markets.

Furthermore, we have numerous innovative features such as 'incoming bets', 'betting performance tracker' and 'bet upsell' features, to name a few.
