Welcome to Coolbet, a gaming site that strives to be fair, personal and transparent. Coolbet is a gaming site and sportsbook software, with 200+ employees, most of them working from our head office located in the center of Tallinn, Estonia. We hold gaming licenses in Estonia, Sweden and Malta.

Coolbet is all about momentum and innovation, and that’s why we are strongly focused on product development and setting new standards for responsible gaming. We work hard every day to provide quality in services, products and, of course, execution.

The name Coolbet was chosen for two reasons. Firstly, because it’s short and impossible to forget. Secondly, because it represents our motto- “Stay Cool and Bet Responsibly”.

Our management brings over 90 years of online gambling experience to the table and they have assembled staff that is second to none in the industry. That’s why we give you, our customer, the very best service in everything we do.
