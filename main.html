How to use?

One Cool brand, One Cool team!

A strong brand brings with it trust and acceptance. That’s why our customers need to have the same experience regardless of which country they are in at any given moment.

The Coolbet marketing system is a compilation of all Coolbet guidelines. Here you can find directions for branding and design (landing pages, banners, print, merch, etc).

You also have access to our source materials, codes, and examples of how to apply/not apply the guidelines.


So, go through the menu and remove your doubts :)


Is something missing? Let us know.
  • if it’s about visuals, at design@coolbet.com
  • If it’s about overall branding, at karoliina@coolbet.com

What is new?

If there is something new or if any change has been made, the marketing team will update the materials.
