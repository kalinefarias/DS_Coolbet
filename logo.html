Logo

The Coolbet logo is fun, catchy, and recognizable. It consists of the four primary colors, the company´s name, and a colorful bear icon.

Clearance - Minimum

To ensure that logo is clearly visible, surround it with clear space that is free of type, graphics or other elements that might cause visual clutter.

Use the capital "C" of the Coolbet wordmark to define the minimum clearance around the logo.


Logo Colors

Pantones:
  • Green - PANTONE 360 C , (DARKER) PANTONE 7738 C
  • Blue - PANTONE 306 C, PANTONE 7706 C
  • Orange - PANTONE 715 C, PANTONE 7414 C
  • Red - PANTONE 485 C, PANTONE 7621 C

Colorful and monochrome color

The first choice is always a colorful logo on a dark background. Don’t place a logo on a mixed colors background. The logo should be fully visible at all times. If the background is light (white, light grey etc) use a monochrome logo, preferably black one.

SAMPLES (one also where there is super-colorful background and then colorful logo on it, so the logo is not well visible)

Logo sizes

To keep the logo clear and easily readable rules for minimum sizes were created.

Dont's

  • Change the logo as you want. We have one style logo and we will stick to it.
