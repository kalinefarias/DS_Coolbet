Coolbet merchandise has a duty to represent our brand and represent it well by reflecting our brand values faithfully, creatively and consistently. We want to give out well branded products which our customers would wear and use with pride.

The following guidelines will help you to understand the principles behind our merchandise, as well as outline the practical considerations and technical details for creating and producing new items. Please use them when producing new merchandise and whenever you have second thoughts if your idea lands between guidelines, contact Karoliina.


Principles and Considerations

Each item has to be chosen carefully!

We offer quality, so the item has to be made from quality materials and the branding has to meet the standards.

  • Good quality items and branding will look the same even after 50 washes or daily touching.
  • If choosing to brand technical items - test them before ordering.

Coolbet merchandise reflects the message “We want to make your life easier, so that you can feel comfortable while having fun”.

Stay true to the message when choosing a product. Always question whether an item is necessary—what does it add to a customer’s life? Is it likely to have a life beyond your event, or will it immediately end up in the trash can?

We should avoid devaluing our brand by placing our logo on items that have negative connotations around their use—for instance, a doormat that will be walked on, a candy wrapper that will be thrown away, or napkins that will be used to clean up a mess.

Logo

Embroidered logo on clothing - stays nice longer and print on other products.

There’s a difference between branded and corporate merchandise. Use branded merch for specific campaigns/events/occasion and corporate merch for all year around give outs.

Branded merch is more special and also makes people feel more special. They were there, they are part of something nice that has happened.

For branded merch you can use accent colours, just like used for campaign/event visuals.

Color

Our color palette should be used carefully across our merchandise.

Check our colors here

Color Limits

Coolbet character comes out the best when a colorful logo is on black item. 95% of the times this should be the way to go, 5% of the time is exception:

  • When thinking about making merchandise in some other color than black, prefer our Brand colors (see page …) and embroider or print/press the monochrome logo in the same color as the product itself.
  • You also have an option to still make black merch and brand it with black monochrome logo. This will make the product look quite exclusive.
  • If the occasion needs white merchandise, use the colorful logo on black square background.

Patterns

Our approved patterns are a decorative element that adds vibrancy to plain surfaces, helping enliven our merchandise.

Super limited edition, for some specific occasion you can also make branded merch with Coolbet artsy picture. Would be better if you make “a collection“, for example a hoodie where you have an artsy pic on the back + cap where you have an artsy pic on and a phone cover-cardholder with an artsy picture.

Don'ts

  • Don’t choose items which can be easily thrown away or damaged or trampled on.
  • Don’t brand items which doesn’t have any connection with our brand values or serve the purpose “We want to make your life easier, so that you can feel comfortable while having fun”
  • Don’t make something just to have it
  • Don’t brand an item which you personally think is a cool color but doesn’t represent Coolbet.
  • Don’t mix the patterns when making branded merchandise. Use one pattern in one size.
  • Don’t make the logo too big. Use the provided measurement guidelines.
  • Don’t add random text in a random font. The text you add on Coolbet merchandise is either our slogan or carries some other important purpose, like a winner or occasion name.
